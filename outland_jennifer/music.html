Music

 

All of Liberace's Pianos have a purpose, each piano is different from another and distinct. If you are involved in the tour or even wondering on your own, information about each piano is located right next to the pianos to provide more education on them.

Broadway Piano one of the oldest in existence made the year of 1788, the pedals are located at the end of the front leg supports.

The Mirrored Baldwin Grand Piano, this specific piano was made especially for Liberace. One just like it remains in the Smithsonian in Washington, DC.

Made in Paris in the 1800's The Chopin Piano also seen in the movie " Song To Remember." The exterior and interior is painted in French style on the lid.

Steinway Square Grand Piano made the year 1863 at the Steinway Company of New York. This Piano is special because it incorporates Rosewood and African Ivory Keys, Silver Hinges, and an original gold leaf logo.

Liberace's pianos are made long before our time, so the make is different. Liberace as talented as he was used these complex pianos to make beautiful music for entertainment.
