Cars

 

Liberace is famous for his very stylistic vehicles, the picture represent his and his preference in cars.

Liberace purchased a 1917 Volkswagen Rolls Royce, Jr. which was customized to resemble a Rolls Royce. It was painted white and later painted pink to match his pink-feathered costume. This car was even used on stage to carry out his cape during his concerts. If you go to the clothing page you can see the matching costume.

Duesenberg Roadster Replica, this car was adorned with thousands of rhinestones to match his Ice Blue Costume and his rhinestone Baldwin grand piano for Liberace's finale during his performances at Radio City Music Hall in 1986.

1972 Custom Bradley GT was patterned after the "Gull-Wing" Mercedes Benz, had a Volkswagen engine, and has gold flecks in the molding. Liberace drove this sports car to California.
