Clothing

 

Liberace is famous for the heavy clothing he wears when performing. I thought I would display the most elaborate outfits he wore because they are beautifully made. His ensembles included very small rhinestones, feathers and vibrant colors.

Some of his famous pieces match his pianos as well as his cars, he enjoyed this flamboyant look.

Some of the clothing displayed in the photos are the Hot Pink Jacket, this comes with the whole ensemble pants, matching vest, tie, and cape. The first time Liberace wore the outfit was the year of 1984 at World's Fair in New Orleans.

The Purple Costume he wore at the Radio City Music Hall in New York City, 1984.

Red, White, and Blue Hot Pants with a cape, Liberace is famous for wearing this on the 100th anniversary for the Statue of Liberty, however, instead of holding a torch like the statue he held a Candelabra.

King Neptune costume is the heaviest non fur costume out of them all, weighing 200 lbs, he wore this outfit at the famous Caesars Palace in Las Vegas Nevada.
