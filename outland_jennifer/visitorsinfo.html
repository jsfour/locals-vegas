Visitors Info

 

 

Group tours are available at the Liberace Museum. Bring as many as fifteen people or more.

Tours: 11:00am-2:00pm

The Cafe offers a variety of different food items and beverages to snack on during your tour.

The Gift shop is available before or after your tour at the Liberace Museum, so bring home a memory of Liberace today.

Group Events, birthdays, and corporate events are welcome to join Liberace's unique banquet space to have special private events or parties.

Contact Us:

Liberace Museum is located on 1775 E. Tropicana Ave. (@ Spencer) Las Vegas Nevada 89119-6529.

Visiting Hours:

Mondays-Closed

Tuesdays 10:00am-5:00pm

Sunday 12:00pm-4:00pm

Prices:

$12.50 for adults

$8.50 for seniors 65+ as well as students with a valid school ID.

Museum Members and children age 10 or under no charge. Children must be accompanied by an adult.

If you arrive at the Museum by taxi or bus, show your receipt or transfer ticket at the register desk to receive $2.00 off the adult ticket price.
