Liberace Foundation

" A lot of good things have happened to me in show business and I want to do what I can to give others just starting out a career boost. I hope the Foundation projects will continue into the future to offer gifted newcomers financial help, and in many cases, artistic exposure as well." Liberace

 

Liberace Foundation since 1976 funded more then 1,500 individual scholarships in the arts at more then 100 nation's premier arts institutions.

Sponsoring four concerts to benefit the University of Nevada, Las Vegas Music Department Scholarship Fund.

Liberace Foundation funded its first major scholarship series, awarding 10,000 dollars to five learning institutions across the country.

Liberace has awarded close to $5 million in scholarship grants to more then 100 of the nation's most talented institutions in music, theater, dance, and visual arts.
