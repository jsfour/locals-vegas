Yoga Basics - Perfect for anyone who has never done yoga. This gentle class will help you to gain a better understanding of yoga, while relaxing you at the same time.

Level 1 - Great for the begginer who wants to learn more about alignment, form and building a strong foundation of postures for a healthy practice.

Level 1&2 - These classes were designed for the continuing begginer and the intermediate student. The class is faster paced and introduces more challenging yoga postures.

Level 2&3 - Build heat, stamina, and burn calories in these rigorous classes. Enjoy challenging yoga postures while taking your practice to the next level.

Candlelight Yoga - Enjoy our relaxing atmosphere as we practice yoga by candlelight. The soft music and lightning create a serene enviornment to relieve stress and anxiety, while moving through various poses.

Align & Flow - Increase your knowledge of correct body alignment while enjoying the flowing movement of postures. These classes have a mix of Anusara and Iyengar yoga styles.

Yoga With Live Music - Come experience our guest musicians as they share their mind body balancing notes with the class. The candlelight enviornment helps to soothe the body and soul.

Hot Bod Fusion - The most creative hot classes in Las Vegas! Come detoxify your body and mind as you move to progressive music. Be ready to sweat!
