Important Information

Directions:

From Airport: Take 215 West to Charleston. Take a left off the freeway and continue straight until you see the entrance to the park on your right.

US 95N: Take to Summerlin Parkway towards Buffalo and continue to 215 E to Charleston. Take a right and continue straight until you see entrance to park.

 

Hours Of Operation:

 

*Visitor's Center:

8:30 to 4:30. Closed on Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Years Day.

*13 Mile Scenic Loop Drive:

October 1-31: 6a.m. to 7p.m.

November 1 - February 28: 6a.m. to 5p.m.

March 1 - 31: 6a.m. to 7p.m.

Fees:

Red Rock Canyon started collecting fees in 1996 to help keep the park looking its best for your family.

*Daily Fee: $5

*Red Rock Annual Pass: $20

At the entrance of the park you pay to visit and you receive a Visitors Guide filled with all kinds of information. From Upcoming Events to Safety Tips. There are many fun articles you can read also.
