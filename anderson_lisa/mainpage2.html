Red Rock Canyon Park

A Place for Families

If you want to take your family somewhere besides "the strip", The Red Rock Canyon Park is the place to go. Right outside the frantic fast-paced elements of Las Vegas is a beauty few know about. The Red Rock Canyon Park brings families together outside and in nature. Red Rock is a great place to have a family picnic or take an easy to moderate hike on any of the beautiful trails this park has to offer.

 

 vghh

Red Rock also offers a gorgeous loop drive around the park. if you don't want to get out then just go for a drive around the loop. your family can roll the windows down and get some fresh air while maybe taking some family photos along the way. Red Rock Canyon is a natural escape for your family to spend some time together.

If your family really wants to enjoy the park the best time to go early afternoon between the months of march to may and september through October.
