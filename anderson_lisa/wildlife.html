Wildlife

Along many of the trails your family will be able to see some very unique wildlife that you can only see in the desert. There are also many different animals you might be ale to spot if you look really hard.

Here are two very prominent animals to the desert...

There are many different kinds of lizards scurrying their way around the slippery rocks and green bushes. The desert tortoise is one animal not many people see in it's natural habitat because it is becoming extinct. Maybe you will be able to see the states animal, the Bighorn Sheep.

There are also a lot of different kinds of plants and trees that are quite unique because very few plants can survive is such a dry climate as the desert.

hihi

The Prickly Pear Cactus is one that many of us see in pictures or even cartoons. These plants were often cooked because of the sweetest fruits and thick fleshy pads. The pads also offered many forms of pain killer and could even be used to treat infections .

The Banana Yucca is known for its tall banana like structure that grows out from the middle. these are also often used for food because of the sweet seeds. The leaves provided fibers to weave baskets and soap was made from its roots.

Red Rock Canyon is a great place for you family to learn more about the desert and that fascinating things that survive here. So get a pamphlet from the visitors center and take turns reading about the plants and trees you see along the way
