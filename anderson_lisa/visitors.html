Visitors' Center

The Visitors' Center has a lot to offer from a fantastic gift shop to a memorial trail. the gift shop has everything from a nature CD for mom, to a desert coloring book for sister and a stuffed desert tortoise for brother. Dad would enjoy all the different books on the desert and Red Rock Canyon. After buying some memories from the gift shop your family can continue onto the small museum of desert wildlife. your children can see desert animals up close and be able to learn something about them.

 

 hihi

When your family continues outside there is a memorial trail for those lives lost in September 11.

Your family can take a short walk on various tiles with names of those who died serving America. This trail might give your family sometime to remember and discuss your thoughts about that day.

Then your children can look for Mojave Max in a special desert habitat in the center of the memorial trail. Mojave Max is a desert tortoise that all kids love to watch slowly make his way across the desert and munch on weeds along his way.

If your family decides to take a trip to Red Rock Canyon make sure you stop by the Visitor's Center. You can meet Mojave Max and pick up some fun gifts to remember your nature escape right outside the busy Las Vegas city.
