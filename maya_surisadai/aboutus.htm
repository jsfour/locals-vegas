Tim Arnold is the owner and founder of this non-profit organization. He started buying pinball machines from where ever he could get them. Ted came to Las Vegas to "retire" and thought that a good way to give to charity was by letting people play the machines.

After paying the bills, Tim gives the rest to the Salvation Army. He has given them up to $3,169.75 in a month. This contribution is greatly appreciated and just shows how much he cares about Vegas!

With about 200 machines...your bound to find one that you like. It's interesting to see how many points you can get. If your good enough you may be there for hours!
