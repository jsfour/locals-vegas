There is a long history of the Gilcrease family in Las Vegas. When you go to the Sanctuary, you can pick up a bulletin that goes into detail. I'm just going to give you a watered down version of the history of the Sanctuary, taken from the bulletin, and give you some links that you might be interested in.

William Orr (Bill) Gilcrease always had a fondness for birds and other animals. He loved to take care of them and started t raise exotic and domestic birds. He also started taking in animals that were displaced. He dreamed of families being able to come and spend time with the animals he loved so much. So on part of the Gilcrease property, he opened up the Sanctuary. Until his death, he was the driving force behind the refuge for animals. You will often see it referred to as the "bird sanctuary."
