Roasted Eggplant Cannelloni: Thinly sliced, roasted eggplant stuffed with goat cheese and baked in a light tomato sauce.

Shrimp Tempura: Butterflied shrimp dipped in tempura, fried, and garnished with tempura fired green onions and sesame seeds. For your dipping pleasure, a ponzu sauce is provided. Not to worry, it sounds funny, but all it really is a soy sauce glaze.

 

Roasted Pork Empanadas: Tender roasted pork with a bit of kick, stuffed inside a flaky pastry and drizzled with a creamy chipotle sauce. If you like chimichangas, then you'll love this dish.

 

Mango Mojito: All the minty coolness of a regular mojito, infused with some mango madness. Firefly's specialty drink is the sangria, but every once in a while, it's fun to live on the wild side.
