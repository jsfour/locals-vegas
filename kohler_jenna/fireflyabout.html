Tapas (TAH-pas): Name for a wide variety of appetizers in Spanish cuisine. They can be served cold or warm and are traditionally given for free with a drink prior to lunch or dinner.
Bar: A business that sells alcoholic beverages for on site consumption. Over the years, bars have also become places to for people to socialize with friends and meet new people.
In a January 2007 issue of 944 Magazine, part owner of Firefly, John Simmons told the magazine about his original vision of Firefly, "What I really wanted was to open a little place for locals to stop after work, maybe have some small plates, a couple drinks and enjoy themselves before they head home."
