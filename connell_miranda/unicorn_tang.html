Unicorn Tang

The Unicorn Fish is found in the Indo-Pacific regions of the world.  They prefer to live in areas with strong surge,with small groups. The Unicorn Tang feeds on coarse leafy brown algae.  Adult males tend to have better developed horn, peduncular spines, and caudal filaments than females of the same size.

Tangs are members of the Surgeonfish family.  These fish have long continuous dorsal and anal fins and crescent tailfins.  The scalpel at the base of their tailfin is very sharp and is used for protection from predators as well as a way of establishing itself with other fish.
