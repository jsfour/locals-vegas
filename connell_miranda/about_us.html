The Silverton Mermaid Aquarium is a 117,000 gallon artificial reef which is home to over 4,000 tropical fish from all over the world.  There are three different species of stingrays, as well as six different species of sharks. The aquarium is a beautifully designed octagonal aquarium with viewing on all sides. The front viewing window is an impressive convex window that extends 25’ X 12’ and is one of the largest single pieces of curved acrylic in the US.
