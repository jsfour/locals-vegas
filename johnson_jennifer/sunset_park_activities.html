There are plenty of fun and exciting things to take part in at Sunset Park. Here's a list of the year round activities...

  • Basketball
  • Baseball
  • Tennis
  • Swimming
  • Volleyball
  • Jogging
  • Golf
  • Fishing
  • Picnicking
  • Dog Park
  • Horsehoe
  • Frisbee

Sunset Park is home to many events throughout the year. My two favorites are the Gift of Lights, which occurs during the month of December, and the Renaissance Fair in October.

As the sun begins to set and the park cools off, my favorite things to do are to have a picnic, play volleyball and basketball. The park is illuminated with bright stadium lights that make it easy to see. In the evening is when Sunset Park's basketball courts are full of activity.
