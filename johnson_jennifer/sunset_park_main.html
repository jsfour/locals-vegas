Sunset Park, one of Clark County's largest public parks at around 400 acres, is located at the corner of Sunset Rd. and Eastern Ave. Open year-round and full of things to do, Sunset Park has become one of my favorite places to go for recreation.
The park is a great outing for sports enthusiasts, families, and even dogs! Anyone can come out and enjoy taking part in all types of activities, such as picnicking, basketball, tennis, golf, baseball, and volleyball games.
