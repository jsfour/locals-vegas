HISTORY

The first Zia Record Exchange opened in Arizona over 25 years ago. Today there are eight locations in the Southwest.

 

Zia Las Vegas became the first Zia to open outside of Arizona when it opened its doors in May 2005.

 

Future plans include a second Las Vegas location opening as soon as this year!!!
