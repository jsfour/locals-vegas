Campgrounds

 

Campgrounds at both canyons cost around $15.00 per night and offer a great place to relax with picnic tables, a fire pit and flushable tiolets. Tenting has its advantages especially when positioned by a calming stream.

The Kyle Canyon campground is McWilliams and at Lee Canyon there is Dolomite, Fletcher View and Hilltop. The Hilltop campgrounds are unique due to its location which is in between the two canyons on the scenic drive that connects the two. It sits at a higher elevation than the others and offers panoramic views.
In addition to camping, picnicing is also a great way to relax in the wilderness. Prime spots for picnics are Cathedral Rock, Deer Creek, Foxtail and Old Mill. Cathedral Rock and Old Mill cost $3 per car, the others are free entry.
