The Lodge

 

The Mt. Charleston Lodge sits at an elevation of 7,717 ft and is a relaxing and romantic getaway from a busy city. The lodge includes a pond at its entrence, fine dining, an arcade and a lovely deck that has breath-taking mountain views.

 

Room rates range from $150.00- $180.00 Sunday through Thursday and from $215.00-$260.00 on Friday and Saturday.

 

Amenities include wedding areas for both receptions and weddings, dining by fireside and events like piano playing and an outdoor craft show on the weekends during spring.
