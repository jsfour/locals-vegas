Ski and Snowboard Resort at Lee Canyon
The Las Vegas Ski and Snowboard Resort is located in Lee Canyon and is fun for learners as well as novices. The resort is open from November through March. Lovers of the resort can purchase season passes ranging from $170 to $350. The resort offers equipment rentals, lessons and group rates. There is a lodge located at the bottom of the lifts to rest and have some lunch.
