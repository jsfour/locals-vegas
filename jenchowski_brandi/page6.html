Directions to Mt.Charleston
Mt. Charleston is 35 miles away from Las Vegas. The easiest route is taking the 93/95 freeway all the way out past the Indian Springs Reservation and soon after will be a sign to Kyle Canyon or for Lee Canyon just keep going for about 9 more miles. For a map click here.
