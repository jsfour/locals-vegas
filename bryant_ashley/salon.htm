I walked into the salon on a busy Saturday afternoon after having lunch with a friend, determined to get a trim. I wasn't ready for the experience that I had! I was greeted immediately by a chipper girl at the front with the choice of either Aveda Tea or a mini bottle of water while I waited for my stylist in the waiting room.

Once the stylist emerged I was whisked away to the shampoo bowls to get my hair washed. I've had my hair washed before, but this was no joke! My stylist started washing my hair and we began a friendly conversation,when he was done with what felt like a head massage conditioner was applied and a towel was draped over my head (to help the conditioner penetrate my hair).

While I waited for my hair to drink up the yummy smelling conditioner I was treated to a hand massage by one of the assistants. After almost falling asleep in the chair I was escorted over to my stylists station I sat down and talked about the vision for my hair (even though it was just a trim).
After listening carefully to what I had envisioned my hair to look like my stylist gave his suggestions. After coming to a conclusion the cutting began. I'm not sure what was done but I looked "Hott"! I walked out of that salon with an appointment for the next weekend for a color!
