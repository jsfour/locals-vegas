Local Reviews

 

I went into Danielle's and wanted to get some of the publics opinions about Danielle's who already know about this place and whom love it for it's orginality and warm envirnoment. Some qoutes I received were:

"Danielle's White Chocolate Popcorn is to die for. They use to have a location on Decatur, then it closed out of know where and I thought to myself where am I going to get my white chocolate popcorn. It was scary for me at that moment till I dicovered they only moved to Sahara." Allyson Forgey of N. Las Vegas

 

"Danielle's is my favorite place to bring my kids after school. If my kids had a good day at school or recieved good grades for the week, I bring them here for ice cream or they can have their choice of flavored popcorn or even candy sticks or chocolates. They have a small but great variety of treats." Sally Cooper of NW Las Vegas

"This place has a certain sweet smell to it that reminds me of the older days whne I used to go to ice cream parlors, and there is always wonderful customer service here." Bob Carney N. Las Vegas

"I love their White Chocolate Popcorn. I get it every Christmas for my family and friends as their Chirstmas gifts. Nothing can beat that gift." Patti Lind of NW Las Vegas
