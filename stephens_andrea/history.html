History

 

The sugary goodness of Danielle's chocolates has been in the Lee family starting with their ancestors from about 200 years ago.

Danielle's Ice Cream and Chocolate was founded by Calvin and Joyce Lee. They are both descendants from Europe and are both candy candy makers. They then taught their kids how to make the family goodies and they plan on having Danielle's be a long family traditional owned store.

"We have sugar running through our veins" says Fronda Lee ( Daughter of Calvin and Joyce Lee)

Calvin and Joyce named the store after their youngest child Danielle. They thought that was an apporiate name for Americans to rate to but also suitable for the homemade french chocolate they deliever everyday.
