The fishing pond was constructed in 2001 in cooperation between Boulder City and the Department of Wildlife to allow residents and visitors a place to fish besides Lake Mead.

The pond is located uphill from the parking area. It is approximately 3 acres large with a maximum depth of about 15 feet. It is bordered by a paved sidewalk that allows for chairs and other equipment.
