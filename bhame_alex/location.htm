The Boulder City Fishing Pond is located in Veterans Memorial Park. The park entrance is located about 1 mile south of Buchanan Boulevard and Nevada Highway (US 93). An alternate route is available through Boulder City, head about 1/3 mile south of Buchanan Boulevard and Adams Boulevard.

From the main park entrance turn right and follow the road to the parking lot below the fishing pond.
