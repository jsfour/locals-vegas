Two types of game fish species are stocked in the Boulder City Fishing Pond: rainbow trout and channel catfish. Because of the small size of the pond, other game fish species are not stocked.

Rainbow trout are stocked weekly from November through March and channel catfish are stocked monthly from April through October.

Stocking schedules are available from the Lake Mead Hatchery hotline at 702-486-6738.
