AWARDs

After only being opened for two weeks, Market Grille Café took home the Bite of Las Vegas award for “Best Creative Baklava Cheesecake”.

They had really stiff competition beating out a restaurant who won the same award 9 years running. However, the Aphrodite Cheescake with its smooth rich cheesecake on top of a layer of Baklava, made judges drool at the mouth. So come down and try a slice of this award winning cheesecake.

Market Grille Café has also appeared in many periodicals in the past year. They had stories about the restaurant in the View: Neighborhood Newspapers for Aliente, Centennial, and Summerlin. They have also appeared on Channel 3 News.
