HISTORY

Opened in 2006, The Market Grille Café has become a place of family, friends and great Mediterranean food.

Owners, Joe and Rhonda Pierro are no strangers to Mediterranean food.

Joe is from Buffalo, NY. Joe, who is from Italian decent, was very familiar with good ole’ homemade cooking. He had been in the restaurant industry, managing for more than two decades.

Rhonda is from Greek decent and has always been surrounded by the authentic Greek cuisine. She is originally from Stuebenville, Ohio and moved to Las Vegas in the 1980’s, where she met Joe.

Joe has always wanted to own his own restaurant.  He was use to entertaining family and friends, and knew how to run a restaurant. His desire became a dream come true.

Joe found Mediterranean food to be the best choice for a restaurant because it was something he knew very well and there were a limited number of Mediterranean style cuisines in Las Vegas.

In October 2006, Market Grille Café opened.
Market Grille Café is family owned and run, with authentic Mediterranean cuisine. All the food is made from family recipes passed down through the generations. The Family and friends of Joe and Rhonda, put their heads and creativity together to decorate and design the feeling of a rustic Mediterranean village when you walk through the door.

Market Grille Café’s is known for its family friendly atmosphere, great food and the award winning Bite of Las Vegas Aphrodite’s Cheesecake.
