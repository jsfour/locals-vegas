Capoeira is an art form that was developed in Brazil during the era of slavery. At the time, slaves were not allowed to participate in any form of combat or fight training. Therefore, capoeira was disguised as a form of dance. The movements of the fighters are in tune to the music. The movements are very fluid and in tune with the spirit of the music as well.
