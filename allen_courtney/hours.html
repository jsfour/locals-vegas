Directions

From Las Vegas:

  • Take I-15 North
  • Exit 122 East Mesquite (left off exit ramp)
  • Right on Oasis Blvd
  • Right on Kittyhawk Drive
  • Kittyhawk Drive will take you to the Mesquite Municipal Airport
  • End at Hangar #2 on the left side

Hours of operation

Saturday & Sunday: 9am - Sunset

Monday - Friday: by appointment only. To make an appointment, call

1-877-2GO-JUMP (1-877-246-5867).

Appointments are recommended and get priority over walk-ins.
