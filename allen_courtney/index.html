Have you ever dreamed of flying? What about falling over 10,000 feet at 120 miles per hour? Then you're ready to go SKYDIVING!

Skydive Mesquite is just one hour outside of Las Vegas. Make your jump from over 2 miles above the ground!

Jumpers must be at least 18 years old (absolutely no exceptions) and weigh less than 220lbs.

Skydive Mesquite is a USPA Group Member.

Phone: toll free number 1-877-2GO-JUMP (1-877-246-5867)
