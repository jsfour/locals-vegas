Restaurant

Enjoy some unique and familiar flavors at the restaurant inside the Mt. Charleston Lodge. They offer selections of wild game meats like buffalo, elk, pheasant and ostrich. Some good down home chili is also featured on the menu covered with onions and cheese as well as some delicious appetizers to start your meal off right. No matter what you order, the service and atmosphere make your visit a treat.

Enjoy you food surrounded by the beauty of Kyle Canyon outside on the patio in the summertime. During October enjoy a fabulous Oktoberfest menu as well as special menus for other holidays.

The restaurant also offers catering for any event that you could want to have at the Lodge.
