Things To Do

No matter what season, there is always something to do at the Mt Charleston Lodge, or at least near by.

Wintertime allows guests to sled, tube, snowball fight and just have a great time playing in the snow. Park at the lodge and you are a hop skip and a jump from all the action. When you get too tired from playing, come inside and enjoy our famous hot chocolate or other specialty drinks around our red center fireplace.

You can enjoy shopping in the Holiday Shoppe year-round to keep Christmas living all 12 months. There you will find holiday ornaments, crafts, and many other festive trinkets.

Spring and summertime gives a pleasant place to hike in the Kyle Canyon with many trails starting near the lodge or a short drive away. Thirsty and hungry after your hike? Come back to the lodge for lunch or dinner and a nice cool drink. Stay the night in a cabin and do it again the next day if you’d like.

The canyon is so beautiful year-round that it is a photographer's dream. Bring your camera with you on your hike and get some unbelievable pictures. No one will ever believe that you went to Vegas.
