Mt. Charleston Lodge

Less than 30 minutes outside of Las Vegas is a mountain getaway. The Mt. Charleston Lodge has been an escape from the lights of Las Vegas since 1962 offering food and creative drinks and libations to all visitors.

With temperatures averaging 30 degrees cooler than those in the Las Vegas Valley, it offers a cool retreat during the hot summer months and access to snow during the wintertime.

With the addition of the cabins in 1997 the Lodge offers more than just a day trip escape but a multiple day experience.

Whether you visit for a day, evening or take a weekend stay, make sure you sample the fabulous specialty drinks in the restaurant. There is nothing better than sipping a World Famous Mt. Charleston Coffee while sitting next to the center fireplace inside the lodge.

Located at:

1200 Old Park Road
Las Vegas, NV 89124
