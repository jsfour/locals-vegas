Entertainment

Whether you are entertained by the video poker machines at the bar or by sipping a cocktail by the center fireplace with friends, it is never dull at the Mt Charleston Lodge.

Weekends they offer live entertainment to watch and enjoy with friends and other visitors.

Currently Mt Charleston Lodge is featuring Claudia Jones. Sip on a Peppermint Coffee while listening to Claudia sing standards and jazz classics while sitting behind her white baby grand. Claudia Jones plays Thursday to Saturday nights til Midnight in the Lounge area.

Located at:

1200 Old Park Road
Las Vegas, NV 89124
(702) 872-5408 or (800) 955-1314
