Just as the sign above implies, people die at Red Rock Canyon. There are many dangerous spots along the trials, its important to use caution and proper judgment when hiking any of the trails.

Important to Know!

  • A good pair of hiking shoes could save your life! The shoes you dance in may not be the best shoes to hike in.
  • Your cell phone cannot save your life! In many places you will not get reception. Don't rely on your phone, let others know where you go.
  • There are no water fountains along the trails! Bring your own water, its important to stay hydrated.
  • You are not the only wildlife here! There are many venomous snakes, spiders, and scorpions that live under rocks. Watch where you put your fingers and toes.
