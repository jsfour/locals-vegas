Location
Red Rock Canyon is located just west of Las Vegas. A simple 35 minute drive from Las Vegas.
HCR 33, BOX 55000, Las Vegas, NV 89124
Directions from UNLV

Hours
Oct. 1-31 & Mar. 1-31 6 a.m. to 7 p.m.
Nov. 1- Feb. 28 6 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Fees and Passes
There is a fee of $5 to enter the park, if you plan to come back multiple times you can purchase an annual pass for $20 which allows you to come as many times as you wish throughout the year.

Visitor Center
Open from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. daily. Closed on Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year's Day.
The Visitor Center has a gift shop with books and souvenirs. But don't forget to check out the exhibit area, you can walk through a display section and learn about the history of the Red Rock Canyon Area.

Scenic Drive
There is a beautiful 13 mile scenic drive through the park. From this drive you have access to the many trails and overlooks.

 

Don't Forget
Red Rock Canyon is public land that is there for your enjoyment. Please keep it clean and remember to deposit all wastes in appropriate places!
