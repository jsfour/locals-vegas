Dogs make great companions and family members, take them hiking with you!

All the trails in the park are dog friendly just as long as the four legged hiker is on a leash. There are trails for the lazy dogs and trails for the dogs that are better fit than yourself. My dogs, Buddy and Bandit (pictured on this site), love to go hiking at Red Rock, it’s a great time for them to get out and exercise and helps me get off the couch. Many of the trails have rocks and boulders that they love to scramble across and jump from (with caution). If you have a dog you may find bringing them to Red Rock Canyon a fun experience!

This is Buddy, he is a Rat Terrier
This is Bandit, he is a Rat Terrier Mix

Please Remember! Just as you get can get hot, tired and thirsty so can your dog. Make sure you bring water for both you and your dog. There are many places along the trails to provide shade and rest for you hiking companions when they get hot and tired.

Don’t forget that others enjoy hiking the trails also, and no enjoys stepping in dog mess. Please pick up after your dogs, many of the trails provide bags to do so.
