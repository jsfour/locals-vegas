"Ichigo Ichie" (ee-CHEE-GO ee-CHI-EH) is the Naked Fish's slogan. It means there might be only one chance to meet people in our life time, so we have to appreciate the precious time we meet each other.

Naked Fish promises that your dining experience will be full of excitement and the highest quality of food and hospitably.

This famous Japanese proverb was made by a tea ceremony expert, Souji, in 16th century.
