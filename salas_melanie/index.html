~All American Sports Arena~

The all American Sports Arena has 95,000 square feet of FUN for everyone. The building features numerous recreactional activities both indoor as well as outdoor. The Sports Arena is famous for their large slugger stadium of batting cages. The Park also has activites like paintball, boxing, soccer, and many more. There are no other facilities in Las Vegas this size with such a variety of activities.
