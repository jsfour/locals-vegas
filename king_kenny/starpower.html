The Moulin Rouge was the first Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas to allow its black entertainers the opportunity to use the facilites after performing. Some of the artists include:

  • Sammy Davis Jr.
  • Nat King Cole
  • Pearl Bailey
  • Louis Armstrong

Many white performers performed at the hotel and casino as well. It was not uncommon to see many of these people gambling after a show:

  • George Burns
  • Jack Benny
  • Frank Sinatra
