The Moulin Rouge has seen its share of owners over the past 50 years. Among those, notable owners have been:

  • Boxing Heavyweight Champion Joe Louis
  • The first African American woman to hold a Nevada Gaming License, Sarann Preddy-Knight
  • The Moulin Rouge Development Corporation (purchased the Moulin Rouge in 2004 for $12 million and announced a $200 million dollar renovation project)
