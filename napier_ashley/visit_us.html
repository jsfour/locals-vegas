Visit Us

For those who are familiar with the Green Valley area, the factory is located at Sunset and Sunset. Head east, make your second left (there are handy signs posted), and it’s at the end of the street.

The factory and Cactus Garden are open daily from 8:30 am to 7:00 pm.

Admission is free, and tours are self-guided.

Special guided tours may be arranged for parties of 10 or more by calling (702) 435-2655.
