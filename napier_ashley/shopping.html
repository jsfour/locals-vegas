Shopping

Like all good factories, the Ethel’s Chocolate Factory provides visitors with opportunities to shop.

At the beginning of your tour, browse around the M&M store to find some cute souvenirs featuring your favorites M&M's characters.

To purchase online, visit M&M's World here.

After your walk down the hallway where all the magic happens, you can buy the tasty end products in the Ethel’s lounge. Stop and enjoy some chocolate fondue with endless dippers, or maybe some hot chocolate on those cold winter days and nights.

To purchase online, visit Ethel's Chocolate here.
