My Tour

Having gone through the entire school system here in Las Vegas, I’ve been to the Ethel’s Chocolate Factory countless times for not only school field trips, but also for the free samples they give!

I recommend beginning by browsing through the M&M store. If you or anyone you know like M&Ms, then you’re sure to find some cute gifts or souvenirs here. You might want to hold off on buying it until you’re ready to leave, though, because there’s still a lot to see.

When you’re done browsing, start your walk down the actual factory part of the tour. Take your time and read all about the history of the company and the history of chocolate in general.

When you’re done here, look at all of the tasty chocolates in the Ethel’s lounge. There is a wide variety of flavors to try and a great selection of bars, pops, and boxes for you to take home with you. Remember that you can customize your own box for the same price as a pre-pack. I definitely recommend the fondue, as well. Though I have not tried any of the flavored ones, the milk chocolate is quite tasty and they give you endless amounts of the dippers to go with your fondue!

After you indulge, take a walk through the cactus garden and see the variety of plants that call our desert home. It’s a nice break from being on-the-go all the time.
