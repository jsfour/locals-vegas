Cactus Garden

The Botanical Cactus Garden at the Ethel’s factory is a nice break from the hustle and bustle of the city that never sleeps.

As the state's largest collection of its kind, the Cactus Garden encompasses four acres of cacti. The Garden is based on the English landscape model of naturalistic desgin and home to over 300 species of plants.

Featuring long winding paths and benches throughout, the cactus garden is a good place for visitors to either take a load off or walk off all of the chocolate they just enjoyed.

If you love Christmas, then you’ll find kindred spirits here from the middle of November through the end of December. The cactus garden is extravagantly decorated with Christmas lights and truly brings the Christmas spirit to life. The factory holds special extended hours during this period, so come see the lights and enjoy some yummy hot chocolate!
