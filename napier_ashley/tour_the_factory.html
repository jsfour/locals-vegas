Tour the Factory

The Ethel’s Chocolate Factory is the production site for all of the Ethel’s chocolates.

The free, self-guided tour of the factory allows visitors to view the machinery used from behind  glass in a very long hallway. Workers are most often there on Mondays to fill orders from the stores, but can also be seen hard at work on other days throughout the week, excluding Sunday.

The hallway also serves as the connector between the M&M store at the beginning and the Ethel’s store at the end. Along the way, visitors learn fun chocolate facts, quotes from celebrities about the joys of chocolate, and are treated to views of elaborate pieces created entirely from chocolate.
