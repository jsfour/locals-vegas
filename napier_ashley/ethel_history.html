Ethel's History

The Ethel’s Chocolate line evolved from the Ethel M Chocolate line that pleased both young and old for 26 years.

Ethel M, as you may know, was created using the recipes of Ethel Mars, mother of Forrest Mars, Sr., the founder of the Mars Corporation.

Ethel’s is a new take on the premium chocolate experience, providing chocolate lovers with a more European taste and offering flavored fondues and drinks, such as various teas and hot chocolate.

Ethel’s is still owned and operated through the Mars Corporation.
