A poetry experience for the urban Las Vegas local.

The Purban Poetry Pulpit is an alternative to going to a movie or a concert. It gives audience members the chance to hear people share experiences that may mirror their own in an artistic format. I think it would be a great place to take a date because the conversation topics are basically provided for you. Plus, the cost of admission includes a free drink or appetizer.

"Since culture is what Las Vegas needs, MHP Entertainment is here to provide a sensational experience of poetry, spoken word and versification from the sacred pulpit of poets. MHP will bring together the urban, suburban, ghetto, classy, sassy, silly, smart, soulful and intellectual to share from their minds, hearts, spirits, emotions, and feelings through words engineered with creativity: spoken!" -Message from MHP Entertainment.
