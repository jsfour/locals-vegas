DIRECTIONS

Paradise Park is located on Tropicana Ave. and Mc Leod Dr.

Mc Leod Dr. is between Eastern Ave. and Pecos Blvd.

Directions from UNLV: Travel east on Tropicana Ave. Go past Eastern Ave. Mc Leod Dr. is the second light after Eastern Ave. Make a left and the park is on your left.
