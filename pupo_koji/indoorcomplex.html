Paradise Park and Recreation Center provide a indoor complex for anyone who wants to use it. This complex includes a basketball court, workout room with cardio equipment and weights, a gymnastic room, a room to teach karate, a daycare center, vending machines, and a quite area. Martial arts classes, tumbling classes, and fitness classes are provided in the Recreation Center. Daycare and youth programs are available during the summer. Recreational basketball leagues are held throughout the year and can be joined upon sign up.
