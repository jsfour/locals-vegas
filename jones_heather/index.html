Mt. Charleston, a beautiful escape from the fast paced Las Vegas strip. Located northwest and about a hour away, the mountain provides a retreat from the heat during the summer and a snowy adventure during the winter. Two of the mountain's most popular hikes are Mary Jane Falls for novice hikers and Cathedral Rock for more experienced hikers. Dogs on leashes are allowed on both hikes. However, smaller toy dogs have difficulty on Cathedral Rock due to the length and incline of the hike. Be sure to check the mountain's current weather conditions to avoid thunderstorms and heavy winds which will make the hikes extremely dangerous. Late spring, summer and fall are the best seasons to enjoy all the mountain has to offer.
