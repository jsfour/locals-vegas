Putting Course

The putting course consists of 18 fun-filled, twist and turn putting holes that will allow you and your friends, family, or date to have fun working on that crucially important putting game. It is a place you can even go alone to work on that putting game. By the end of the full 18 hole course your short game will be significantly better than before, without a doubt.
