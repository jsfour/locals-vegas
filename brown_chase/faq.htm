FAQ's

-Do I need to set up a tee-time in order to play the Cloud 9 golf course?

-For optimum golf experience, yes, due to the fact that you will be guaranteed a time and you will avoid having to wait as a "walk-in" customer.

-Can I rent clubs, shoes, or other golf items at the club house?

-Yes, the club house provides rental clubs for varying fees depending on the types of clubs needed. Official golf shoes are not required, so Angel Park does not offer them as rental items. Golf club rentals must be purchased with a credit card for insurance purposes if you should damage or lose the golf clubs.
