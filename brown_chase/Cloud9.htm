Cloud 9 Golf Course

Cloud 9 is a twelve hole golf course, consisting solely of Par 3 length holes.The hole that is pictured to the left is the most famous hole, number 10. It is an island hole, where golfers must land their ball on the green in order to get a good score.

Prices:

$17 per person (resident), $25 (non)

$10 per person, per cart
