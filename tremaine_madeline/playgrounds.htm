The dino dig is a fun place for the kids to use their imaginations. There is a large pit of sand for them to dig in and pretend like they are finding buried dinosaur fossils.

DINO DIG

This is a childs paradise! Let them enjoy a wonderful day playing with the dinosaurs! Whether they love to go down tall, winding slides, swing high on the sweing sets, run around through water that comes out of the dinosaur, or just take pictures with the statue they are bound to have loads of fun. There is something for everyone at this park and the kids will want to spend hours just hanging out and playing.
