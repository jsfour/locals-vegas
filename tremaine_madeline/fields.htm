If you want to take your family out for a picnic on a beautiful day there are all kinds of gazebos spaced out over the park for you to sit. The gazebos are also great for throwing birthday parties!

If conventional sports aren't your thing, there are shuffle board courts and horse shoe courts just for you!
In terms of space available for playing sports there is a large amount of land that is set aside for soccer. There is also a tennis court that allows two games to be played at one time. Finally if vollyball is your thing there are sand courts ready to be used.
