Located near the entrance into Aliante, the Nature Discovery Park is a great new edition to North Las Vegas. The park sits on 20 acres of land and features:

  • Man-made lake and waterfall
  • Walks and pathways
  • Multi-story play equipment
  • Adventure discovery playground
  • Soccer fields
  • Tennis courts
  • Vollyball courts
  • Gazebos
  • Picnic sites
  • Amphitheater

The Park is located at :

2627 Nature Park Drive
