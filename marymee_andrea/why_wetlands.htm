Personally, I enjoy going to the wetlands park because it makes me forget that I'm in Las Vegas.

I can relax and exercise while forgetting about the busy life of the city that's only a few miles away.

I suggest that everyone take the time to visit the Clark County Wetlands Park at least once. I hope this one visit will lead to many more!

I'd suggest going to the wetlands park early in the morning or later in the evening to avoid the heat.

Either way, make sure to wear your walking shoes as well as bring plenty of water to drink, there is only one water fountain in the Nature Reserve located at the entrance of the Nature Reserve. Water in the Nature Reserve is not intended for human contact, so there is no swimming, wading and fishing allowed.

Also, remember no bicycles or dogs allowed on the Nature Reserve trails, but feel free to bring them on the Duck Creek Trail.
