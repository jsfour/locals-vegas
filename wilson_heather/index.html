The Casablanca

Mesquite, NV

The Casablanca is located about one hour north of Las Vegas off the I-15. The CasaBlanca Resort-Casino-Golf-Spa offers everything that Las Vegas casino hotels do, at more affordable prices and without the congetstion that comes with the Las Vegas Lifestyle.

This peaceful and relaxing resort features a spacious casino floor, a championship golf course, world-class spa facilities, fine dining and nightly entertainment. With everything Las Vegas Casinos have to offer and more, it is easy to see why the Casablanca is the perfect choice for a relaxing and fun vacation.
