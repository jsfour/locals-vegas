Location & Directions
 
 

- Located at 221 North Rampart blvd.

-Coming from the Airport? There are two ways to get to the casino coming from Henderson.

The first way is take the I-15 north to U.S. 95 north. Take U.S. 95 north to Summerlin Parkway West. Take Summerlin Parkway West and exit off Rampart Blvd. Make a left at the light and the Rampart Casino will be on your right hand side.

The other way is to take the 215 west all the way to Summerlin Pkwy. Make a right onto Summerlin Pkwy East. Take this until the Rampart Blvd. exit on the right hand side. Get off on the exit and make a right turn onto Rampart Blvd. The casino will be on the right hand side.
