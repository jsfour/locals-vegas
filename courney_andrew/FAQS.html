How do I get to Spedini's from inside the casino?

If you are entering from the parking garage, go to the elevator and get off on the floor that says casino. When you get off the elevator make a right, go past the slots and tables to the buffet. When you go past the buffet and the Irish pub, walk up the ramp/stairs and Spedini's will be on the right hand side.

How should I dress to dine at this restaurant?

Clothing suitable for Spedini's should consist of a shirt and tie for males, and formal for females.
